ABOUT ME

BUCHÓN Bloom

Where sentiment blossoms into elegance

What is this so called flower business? My business is called Buchón Bloom. I am a florist that also makes money bouquets for people. The way I manage this business is through social media and word of mouth. I founded this business on November 6th, 2023. Currently, I am located in Titusville, FL.

I came up with the idea from making a money bouquet for my friend’s birthday and from that moment I wondered what could be done with this to make a business!!

My mission for Buchón Bloom is to be able to tailor and create bouquets to one's imagination! Do you want to be a part of that mission?
